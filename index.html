About Us: Neuromuscular Physical Therapy of WNY is an outpatient clinic that treats patients with a variety of neuromuscular and vestibular disorders with skilled physical therapy intervention. Patients will be evaluated and treated based on their specific needs through therapeutic exercise, manual therapy, patient education, use of therapeutic modalities and more. Patients will receive one on one care with skilled physical therapists to progress toward their functional and mobility goals using the current evidence-based treatment practices and cutting-edge equipment and technology.

Our Mission: Neuromuscular Physical Therapy of WNY will provide the best neurorehabilitation through:

  • Quality of life and functional training
  • Best practice through current evidence
  • Health and wellness
  • Patient education

Physical Therapy Services:

  • Patient and family education
  • Manual therapy
  • Therapeutic modalities
  • Therapeutic exercise
  • Neuromuscular/functional retraining
  • Adaptive equipment evaluation
  • Gait training
